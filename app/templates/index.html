Cultivating Culture

The Cascades Tech Club was founded to provide students and our community with a social network of individuals interested in technology. The club was founded in Spring 2016 by a small group of students seeking to teach, learn, and grow in the Computer Science program at OSU Cascades as well as the growing tech community in Bend.

We are open to all individuals who are interested in learning some new tech as well as anyone who is looking to experience the culture at OSU Cascades. During the school year, we host regular meetings every Monday at 12:00pm in Tykeson Hall as well as semi-regular workshops, guest speakers, and other fun events.

Whats Trending?

We are dedicated to exploring as many of the new trends in the industry that we can. We aim to provide our community with a fun place to learn about anything of intereset. From simple HTML & CSS to Embedded Systems programming, there is no tech topic that we can't explore together.

Join Our Discord Server

The members of the Cascades Tech Club utilize a chat service called Discord as their main communication tool. There is almost always someone online to help in any way that they can. Club members use Discord to ask questions, collaborate in groups, as well as general chat. the CTC Discord server has grown to over 100 members and is a valuable resource when seeking help.
